Yes! ReelSave is 100% free. There are no limits on downloads, no registration required, and no hidden charges.
Can I download private Instagram Stories? +
ReelSave can only download content from public accounts. Private account content requires the account owner's permission.
What format are downloaded videos saved in? +
All videos are saved in MP4 format, which is compatible with all devices and platforms including iPhone, Android, Windows, and Mac.
Is it safe to use ReelSave? +
Absolutely. We don't ask for your Instagram login credentials, and we don't store any of your data or downloaded content on our servers.
